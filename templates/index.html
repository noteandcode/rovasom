Rovasom is a semi-automation tool for writing blog posts, articles or essays based on facts on the Internet. No plagiarism. Rovasom is a tool for those who like thinking and hate copy and paste. You can use Rovasom in 6 easy steps.


1. Figure out your topic.

2. Search sources in the topic.


3. Figure out your point of view and your own way to organize the facts you have found in your sources.


4. Copy and paste the urls of the good sources to the Rovasom boxes and press the submit button.

5. Ask questions from Rovasom robot that uses AI to answer your questions. The base of answers are the content on the sites you put their urls into the boxes.

6. Compose your article based on the answers of Rovasom robot.

Try it now. It is free.
